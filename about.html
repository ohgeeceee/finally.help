finally.
The stuff everyone assumes you already know. No jargon. No judgment.

Why this exists

Most explainers either talk past what you need or strip a topic down to nonsense. finally.help gives you one clean everyday analogy, a couple of plain-word definitions, and a one-line takeaway you can actually remember — written for a grown-up who feels behind, never in a way that talks down to you.

How it works

Browse a growing library of explainers, or type any topic and get a fresh one on the spot. Reading, quizzing, and writing your own explainers earns points and unlocks little games — because the best way to learn something is to come back tomorrow and do it again.

Under the hood

A static site on Cloudflare Pages with a small set of serverless functions and a Cloudflare D1 database for accounts and comments. Explanations come from a single LLM call wrapped server-side, so the API key never reaches your browser. Passwords are salted and hashed; the source is open.
