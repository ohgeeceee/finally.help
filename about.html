about

finally.help is a single-purpose tool: paste something confusing, walk away with an explanation a 10-year-old could follow.

why

Most “explainers” either over-talk past what you need or strip a topic down to nonsense. We aim for one clean analogy, two or three terms, and a card you can send to a coworker without apology.

how

The site is a static page on Cloudflare Pages. Explanations are generated on demand by a single LLM call wrapped in a Cloudflare Worker, so your API key never ends up in the browser.

who

Built and maintained by ohgeeceee. Open source. MIT.
